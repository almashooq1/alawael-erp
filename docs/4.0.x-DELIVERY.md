## Compliance posture

- **PDPL retention**: 730 days (configurable via `PDPL_AUDIT_TTL_DAYS`)
- **DSAR SLA**: 30-day legal clock documented with a 20-minute operator flow
- **Erasure workflow**: escalation note in runbook (not auto-executed)
- **PII in audit log**: SHA-256 hashed only; raw IDs never stored
- **Cascaded-call disclosure**: full `correlationId`-based reveal in 2 clicks
